Instructions

Concentration, aslo known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-Suijaki, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

This game is just the best. Seriously, this will change your life as it has for millions of others. Prepare for a transcendental experience, my friend.
